Our Programs

Comprehensive interventions to empower and include young people.

Key Interventions

Educational Assistance

Scholarships, materials, and mentorship to support learning pathways.

Advocacy & Rights

Awareness campaigns and policy engagement to uphold rights.

Digital Inclusion

Training, devices, and connectivity to bridge the digital divide.

Health & Well-being

Adolescent health education, SRH services, and referrals.

Social & Recreation

Events and activities fostering community, confidence, and social skills.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Training and support in small enterprises, microcredit, and climate-smart practices.

Strategic Objectives

  • Advocate youth inclusion in decision‑making processes.
  • Facilitate socio‑economic empowerment and self‑sustaining projects.
  • Provide information and services in health, mobility, legal, and education.
  • Integrate disability issues into mainstream development.
  • Create awareness and advocate for disability rights.
  • Foster partnerships for resource access and program improvement.
