About ZYIPT

Who We Are

Founded in 2011, Zimbabwe Youth for Impaired Persons Trust is a community‑based organization run by and for young people with disabilities in Manicaland Province. We bridge gaps in mainstream services through empowerment, capacity‑building, and advocacy.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: An environment where young people can showcase their abilities and fully participate in society.

Mission: Advancing well‑being by equalizing opportunities for proactive engagement in socio‑economic activities.

Our Journey

ZYIPT began when young people with disabilities in Mutare united to address marginalization and create self‑sustainability through tailored initiatives. Registered under Trust Deed No: 000MA641/2011, we hold inclusivity, integrity, transparency, and accountability at our core.

  • Established: 2011
  • Legal: Trust Deed No: 000MA641/2011
  • Location: Manicaland Province, Zimbabwe
